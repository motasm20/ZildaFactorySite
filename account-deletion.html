Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how you can request deletion of your ZILDA Factory game account and all associated data.

What data do we store?

ZILDA Factory uses Google Play Games / Google sign-in (OAuth). We do not store passwords.

We may store the following game-related data:

  • An internal user ID linked to your Google / Play Games profile
  • Your game progress (machines, tiers, currencies, achievements)
  • In-game purchases and unlocked items

How to request deletion

If you want your game account and all related data to be deleted, please send an email to:

E-mail: SUPPORT_EMAIL_HIER

In your email, please include:

  • A short message that you want to delete your ZILDA Factory account
  • The Google account you use to sign in (for example your Gmail address)
  • Optionally: a screenshot of your in-game profile / ID

After receiving your request, we will delete your game data within 30 days. This includes your game progress, in-game currencies and internal user ID.

Important

  • Deleting your ZILDA Factory game account does not delete your Google account itself.
  • To manage or delete your Google account, please use Google's official account management pages.

Questions

If you have any questions about your data or this process, contact us at SUPPORT_EMAIL_HIER.
