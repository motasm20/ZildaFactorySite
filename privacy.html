Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-02-01

1. Who we are

ZILDA Factory is a mobile game developed by an independent developer based in the Netherlands. In this policy “we”, “us” or “our” refers to the developer of ZILDA Factory.

2. What data we collect

  • Game progress data stored locally on your device.
  • Anonymous analytics such as session length and screens used, to improve the game.
  • Player ID from Google Play Games Services for leaderboards and achievements.

3. How we use your data

  • To save your progress and game settings.
  • To show leaderboards and achievements.
  • To detect crashes and fix bugs.

4. Third-party services

ZILDA Factory may use Google Play Games Services, Google Play Billing and Firebase. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Children

The game is not directed at children under 13. If you are under the minimum age in your country, please ask a parent or guardian before playing.

6. Contact

For any questions about this policy, please contact us via the support page.
